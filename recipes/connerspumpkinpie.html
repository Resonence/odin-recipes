Conner's Pumpkin Pie

Pumpkin pie is a traditional dessert made with a spiced pumpkin filling baked in a pastry crust, often enjoyed during autumn and Thanksgiving celebrations for its rich, creamy texture and warm, comforting flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 400mL pumpkin puree
  • ¼ tsp ground ginger
  • ¾ cup evaporated milk
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 9in. / 23cm unbaked deep dish pie shell

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, beat eggs lightly.
  2. Add pumpkin puree, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger and salt; stir until well combined.
  3. Blend in milk.
  4. Pour filling into pie shell.
  5. Bake at 425°F for 15 minutes.
  6. Reduce oven temperature to 350°F and continue baking for 30-35 minutes.
