Alice's Vanilla Slices

Vanilla slices consist of layers of puff pastry filled with vanilla custard cream, topped with icing or powdered sugar for a sweet and creamy dessert.

Ingredients

  • 9x13 casserole dish
  • 1 box vanilla cook pudding
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1 cup milk
  • 35% real whipped cream
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • Graham wafers
  • 1 cup icing sugar
  • 2 Tbsp birds custard powder
  • 2 Tbsp butter, room temperature
  • chocolate sprinkles, (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place graham wafer on bottom of 9x13 casserole dish.
  2. In a bowl, mix vanilla cook pudding, evaporated milk, and milk. Bring to a boil.
  3. Pour over graham wafers, then put in fridge until cool.
  4. In another bowl, whip together whipped cream with sugar. Put on top of previously cooled pudding.
  5. Put another layer of graham wafers on top of whipped cream.
  6. In a third bowl, icing sugar, custard powder, butter, and a splash of milk until mixture is spreadable.
  7. Pour on top of wafers. Put in fridge until cold.
  8. Serve with chocolate sprinkles.
