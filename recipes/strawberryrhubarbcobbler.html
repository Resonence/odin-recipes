Strawberry Rhubarb Cobbler

Strawberry rhubarb cobbler is a delightful dessert featuring a sweet and tangy filling of strawberries and rhubarb, topped with a biscuit-like crust, perfect for showcasing the vibrant flavors of spring and summer.

Ingredients

  • 9x9x2in. pan
  • 3 cups chopped rhubarb
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, sliced
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • ⅔ cup milk
  • ½ cup sugar to 1¾ cup sugar
  • 1½ Tbsp butter
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1½ Tbsp brown sugar
  • 3 Tbsp sugar
  • ¾ tsp cinnamon
  • 3 tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. In the ungreased pan, combine rhubarb, strawberries and 1¾ cup sugar. Place in oven to warm.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine flour, 3 Tbsp sugar, baking powder and salt.
  4. Pour oil and milk into measuring cup; without stirring, add dry ingredients all at once.
  5. Stir just until dry ingredients are moistened.
  6. Drop dough by spoonful on to hot fruit from oven.
  7. Dot each spoonful with butter.
  8. Top with mixture of brown sugar and cinnamon.
  9. Bake for 25 minutes or until nicely brown.
